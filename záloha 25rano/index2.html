who we are

We are young dynamic company recently founded around group of passionate IT professionals and electro engineers. During designing and developing new technologies we utilize years of experience gained from the world of UT and industry. We are focused on the applications and systems development for industrial companies. In cooperation with them we are preparing innovative solutions which make their lifes easier in following areas:

Health & Safety

We increase safety in industry by using advanced models of artificial intelligence. Fast technological development of image recognition and neutral networks enables the implementation of solutions that were previously difficult to imagine.

Infrastructure & IOT

We design automated systems for remote management of devices and equipment. Even in the event of a power failure or loss of connectivity.

Application development

We develop applications for corporate sphere. In their design, we emphasise user friendliness, modern appearance and simplicity of implementation. Our first product is the Bookley meeting room application.
